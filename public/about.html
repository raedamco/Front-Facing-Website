About Raedam

We're on a mission to revolutionize urban mobility by solving one of the most frustrating problems in modern cities: finding parking.

Our Mission

At Raedam, we believe that technology should make life simpler, not more complicated. Our AI-powered parking platform eliminates the stress and wasted time that comes with searching for parking spots in busy urban areas.

By leveraging real-time data, machine learning, and a growing network of smart sensors, we're creating a future where finding parking is as simple as opening an app and navigating directly to an available spot.

Our Story

Raedam was born from a simple frustration: the daily struggle of finding parking in busy cities. What started as a personal pain point has evolved into a mission to transform urban mobility.

Founded in Portland, Oregon in 2018, we've grown from a small startup to a team of dedicated professionals committed to making cities more livable through smart technology. Our journey has been driven by the belief that great technology should solve real problems and improve people's daily lives.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of drivers across multiple cities, with plans to expand our reach and continue innovating in the urban mobility space.
