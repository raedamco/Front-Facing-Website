Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about early access

What is early access?

+

Early access gives you exclusive access to our smart parking platform before it's publicly available. You'll be among the first to experience our technology and help shape its development.

Is early access free?

+

Yes! Joining our early access waitlist is completely free. There's no cost to sign up and no commitment required.

When will I get access?

+

We're rolling out early access in phases. Once you sign up, we'll review your application and send you an invitation as soon as your access is ready, typically within 2-4 weeks.

What cities are supported?

+

We're currently launching in Portland, Oregon, with plans to expand to Seattle, San Francisco, and other major cities throughout 2021.

Can I cancel my early access?

+

Absolutely! Early access is completely optional and you can opt out at any time. There are no penalties or commitments.
